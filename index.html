What is Smartcare

SmartCare is a mobile personal health record application that empower user with accessibility, mobility and security to their medical health record so that it can be accessed anytime and anywhere. Additionally, we want user to have better understanding of their health and caregivers on the other hand can have better insight of their patient -even before their visit .
